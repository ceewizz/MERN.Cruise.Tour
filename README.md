# MERN.Cruise.Tour
Project 3: MERN Stack Single-Page Application

## Description
This project is to build an application for people who loves the ocean. Travel the sea and go on cruise tours from 4 Days Tour Baja Mexico to 7 Days Euro Trip Tour. With Cruise Tour, you can travel with a group of friends, family members, soulmate, partner, your bestie, or just simply go on your own with other tourists where you will spend a 4 Days - 7 Days exploring some of the best views from Baja Mexico Tours to Europe Tours, dining at buffet on the 5 star cruise ship, and unforgettable trip on the ocean, isolate yourself from daily lives on land.  Travel tickets, buffet tickets, rooms are included in the package price.  All prices are are not included with tax, applicable fees and any additional service fee.
